Welcome

Welcome to Venkateshwara Rice Mill. We are involved in the preparation and sale of high-quality groundnut oil and coconut oil. Customers can bring their own rice, wheat, millets, chilli powder, coriander powder,for fresh powder preparation, while our oils are produced in-house. All products are processed hygienically ensuring quality and trust.

About Us

Venkateshwara Rice Mill was established in the year 2003. Since then, we have been a trusted agro-based business providing pure and natural food products. We follow traditional preparation methods combined with modern machinery to ensure quality, hygiene, and authentic taste. Our long journey since 2003 reflects our commitment to customer satisfaction, freshness, and consistent quality.
